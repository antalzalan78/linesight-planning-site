Services

What I can do for you

Hands-on planning expertise for factories that need clarity on capacity, backlogs and staffing — without heavy software projects.

Capacity planning & scenario modelling

Monthly and mid-term capacity plans per line and product group — what-if scenarios for demand peaks, shutdowns and new SKUs.

S&OP and supply planning support

Support for your S&OP cycle: demand-supply balancing, backlog recovery plans, and clear inputs for management decisions.

Line performance & OEE analysis

Understand where capacity leaks away: OEE breakdowns, changeover impact and realistic run-rate assumptions per line.

Lightweight planning tools

Practical, browser-based planning tools and dashboards tailored to your factory — no license fees, no IT project required.

Interim planning support

An experienced planner stepping in when you need one: parental leave, peak seasons, vacancies or system transitions — up and running in days, not months.

About

Planning experience from the factory floor up

Linesight Planning is the independent consultancy of a supply-chain professional with over 10 years of hands-on capacity and production planning experience in FMCG manufacturing in the Netherlands.

I've spent my career where the plan meets the production line: balancing demand against real line capabilities, OEE, changeovers, maintenance windows and workforce availability. That's the perspective I bring to every engagement — practical, numbers-driven, and grounded in how factories actually run.

Registered as a ZZP (independent contractor) in the Netherlands, available for projects on-site or remote.
